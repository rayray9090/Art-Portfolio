Lust

36x48 | $700

"Lust" delves into the essence of desire and the electrifying allure of sensuality. With a graffiti style, it narrates the complexities of human passion. At the core of the painting, a pair of luscious lips, painted in bold red lipstick, exudes a seductive charm, hinting at vulnerability and the abandonment of inhibitions in the pursuit of desire.

Golden drips flow from the lips, representing the effervescent nature of lust and its uncontrollable, surging emotions. The background, resembling a brick wall, juxtaposes "i love you" with the raw, unfiltered emotions expressed in graffiti-style design. "Lust" is a celebration of sensuality and the primal force that unites us. It invites viewers to contemplate the intertwining of love and lust, recognizing the complex layers of human nature that shape our experiences and stories.
