    ABOUT RAY

    Get to Know Me

    I am Ray Levante, an emerging artist whose work is a celebration of culture, color, and the power of storytelling. My artistic journey began on the streets, where I honed my skills and developed a passion for making a social impact through my creations.

    Rooted in a rich blend of Black and Indigenous heritage, my art is a reflection of the power of unity and diversity. Influenced by artists like Keith Haring, Kaws, and Banksy, I create bold abstract mixed media pieces that fuse traditional techniques with contemporary perspectives.

    My commitment to the Black and LGBTQ+ communities amplifies my dedication to using art as a catalyst for change. "The Canvas Speaks," my latest endeavor, transcends canvas, inviting viewers to immerse themselves in narratives that celebrate culture, challenge norms, and evoke emotions. It's a tapestry that speaks to the complexities of identity, the beauty of color, and the universality of human experience.

    I invite you to explore my work and join me on this transformative artistic journey. Thank you for being a part of this creative adventure.
