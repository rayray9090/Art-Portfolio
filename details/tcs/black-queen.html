THE CANVAS SPEAKS

Black Queen

48x60 | $6000

"Black Queen" celebrates the regal essence of a strong, resilient black woman. Through the medium of abstract line art, this painting captures the indomitable spirit and unwavering gaze of the subject. The black female figure, formed with a multitude of brown shades, represents the diverse and complex nature of identity. The bold red lips serve as a symbol of assertiveness and voice, contrasting against the intricate black afro adorned with textural brown strokes that echo the richness of heritage. The golden earrings, delicately rendered in gold paint, are a testament to inner strength and wisdom.

Set against a subdued grey background, the piece draws focus solely on the empowered expression of the Black Queen. The direct, unwavering gaze transcends the canvas, inviting viewers to reflect upon their own perceptions, biases, and the powerful narratives that shape us all. This artwork stands as a tribute to the resilience, beauty, and grace that define the multifaceted identity of the black woman, inviting contemplation on both the seen and the unseen layers of her existence.
